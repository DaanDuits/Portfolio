Working for Vertigo games in the XR-Lab

For the second quarter of my second year of school I worked at the XR-Lab HLE in Hilversum together with 10 game art students and 11 game development students on a part of the VR shooter After the fall made by Vertigo games. We had to do this in Unreal engine 5 instead of Unity in which After the fall is actually made with While making the game we met 2 developers that worked on After the fall and learned about the bugs the found and struggles they hadden while making the game.

Working with Unreal Engine 5

During this quarter I worked with Unreal Engine for the first time. I did not use the C++ editor yet and used blueprints instead, I still learned alot about the engine and its features. But I honestly don't really like the way alot of components are premade in great detail instead of Unity which activates you to create your own components. However I still want to learn more about the engine and using its C++ editor.

Zombie AI using unreal behaviour trees

The Unreal engine 5 behaviour trees are easy to use and learn. This behaviour tree handles the movement and atacking for the zombies in After the fall - vertical slice. Every time the zombie walks somewhere he checks if he found the player if so he will go there and do his attack animation. Sadly I lost the source project and thus acces to the code files I made.
